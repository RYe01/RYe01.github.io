The First Project

One of my favorite projects evolved from my love for sports, the realization of a problem and the desire to solve it. We often did bodyweight trainings with my friends and we had a couple of favorite spots. Once we were at Lake Balaton looking for a suitable park for street workout. We asked the local teenagers, checked out Google Maps, but found nothing in the neighborhood. Finally, we came across a park, so we didn't have to call off training. That was the moment when I came up with the idea of an application that would help sport enthusiasts in finding a park. This is the story of my website called SWParks Hungary.
