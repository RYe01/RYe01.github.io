Frequently Asked Questions

Q&A

How can I print a document from my laptop at HZ?
  1. Go to print.hz.nl
  2. Login with your HZ username and password
  3. Choose the file that you would like to print
  4. Go to any HZ's printer (either in Middelburg or Vlissingen)
  5. Put your student card on the stand and choose the option 'Print' and pick the file you uploaded

How can I scan a document and send it to my laptop at HZ?
  1. Go to a printer at HZ
  2. Put your HZ studant card on the stand
  3. Put in the paper you would like to scan
  4. Push Scan button on the terminal
  5. Your paper will be sent to your HZ e-mail address

What do I need to do when I am sick / show symptoms of coronavirus?

Stay home and follow the lectures online, it's better for you and others as well. Contact one of the HZ lecturers about your conditions.

How can I book a project space in one of the wings?
  1. Go to HZ Selfservice Portal
  2. Click on 'New Reservation'
  3. Click on 'Book project group rooms'
  4. Pick your time window
  5. Confirm reservation

How can I order my HZ sport card?
  1. Go on your MyHZ profile
  2. Click on 'Webshop' module
  3. Click on 'HZ SPORT' menu
  4. Here you can order your HZ Sport Card with iDeal or Paypal
  5. You can pick it up in HZ (Vlissingen), Room F101 above the bookshop

How can I use public transportation in the Netherlands?

For public transportation you can buy your ticket online, (when it's not COVID season) from the bus driver or by using OV-chipkaart. For the last one you either can have a personal or anonymous card. For personal you need a Dutch bank account because it only can be bought through iDeal. If you want this kind of card you can order it on this site. If you don't have a Dutch bank account yet you can buy these anonymous ones from any automat near train stations, on these devices you can upload it with balance.
